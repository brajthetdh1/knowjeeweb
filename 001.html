What is Force?

Force is a push or pull that can change the state of motion or shape of a body.

SI Unit: Newton (N)

JEE Example Problem 1

JEE Main 2023 Question:

A block of mass 5 kg is pulled with a force of 20 N at 30° to horizontal on a rough surface (μ = 0.4). Find the acceleration of the block.

Solution:

Step 1: Resolve forces

Fx = 20 cos30° = 17.32 N

Fy = 20 sin30° = 10 N

Step 2: Normal force

N = mg - Fy = 5×9.8 - 10 = 39 N

Step 3: Friction force

f = μN = 0.4 × 39 = 15.6 N

Step 4: Net force & acceleration

Fnet = 17.32 - 15.6 = 1.72 N

a = Fnet/m = 1.72/5 = 0.344 m/s²

JEE Example Problem 2

JEE Advanced 2021 Question:

Two blocks of masses 3 kg and 5 kg are connected by a string and pulled with force F on a frictionless surface. If tension in string is 15 N, find force F.

Solution:

Step 1: FBD for 3 kg block

T = m₁a ⇒ 15 = 3a ⇒ a = 5 m/s²

Step 2: FBD for system

F = (m₁ + m₂)a

Step 3: Calculate F

F = (3 + 5) × 5 = 40 N

Step 4: Verification

For 5 kg block: F - T = 40 - 15 = 25 N

m₂a = 5 × 5 = 25 N ✓
