Subject-wise Preparation Strategy

Mathematics Preparation Strategy

Mathematics requires consistent practice and conceptual clarity. Focus on high-weightage topics and develop problem-solving speed.

1 High Priority Topics

  • ✓ Calculus (Differential & Integral) High
  • ✓ Coordinate Geometry High
  • ✓ Algebra (Complex Numbers, Matrices) High
  • ✓ Vector & 3D Geometry
  • ✓ Probability & Statistics

2 Recommended Books

  • Class 11 & 12 NCERT - For basic concepts
  • Cengage Series - For advanced problems
  • Arihant Series - For practice questions
  • Previous Year Papers - For exam pattern

3 Preparation Tips

  • Practice at least 20-30 problems daily
  • Focus on conceptual understanding rather than rote learning
  • Learn shortcut methods for calculations
  • Maintain formula notebook for quick revision
  • Solve previous year papers with time limit

4 Time Allocation

Daily Practice 3-4 hours
Weekly Revision 2-3 hours
Mock Tests 3 hours/week
