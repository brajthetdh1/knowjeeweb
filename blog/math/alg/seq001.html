1. Arithmetic Progression (AP)

üí° AP Problem-Solving Tips

  • If sum of n terms is given as $S_n = An^2 + Bn$, then $d = 2A$ and $a = A + B$
  • For symmetric terms in AP, use the property: $T_k + T_{n-k+1} = \text{constant}$
  • If $p$th term = $q$ and $q$th term = $p$, then $(p+q)$th term = $0$

3. Harmonic Progression (HP)

üí° HP Problem Approach

  • Always convert HP to AP by taking reciprocals
  • Solve the AP problem, then convert back to HP
  • Remember: No direct sum formula for HP - work with corresponding AP

Sum of first n natural numbers $\sum_{k=1}^n k = \frac{n(n+1)}{2}$
