5. Common Scenarios & Recommendations

Scenario 1: Good JEE Rank, Confused About Branch

You have a rank that can get you CS in mid-tier NITs or ECE in top NITs.

üí° Recommendation: Prioritize branch over college

Choose CS in a good college rather than non-CS in a great college, unless you're passionate about that branch.

Scenario 2: Planning for MBA After Engineering

You want to do MBA from IIMs or other top B-schools.

üí° Recommendation: Choose the best brand possible

Top NITs and BITS have excellent placement records in IIMs. Focus on college brand and CGPA.

Scenario 3: Interested in Research & Higher Studies

You plan to pursue MS or PhD after engineering.

üí° Recommendation: Focus on research opportunities

IIITs and top NITs with strong research programs. Look for professors working in your area of interest.
