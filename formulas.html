Mathematics Formulas

Essential formulas for Algebra, Calculus, Coordinate Geometry, and Trigonometry with examples and applications.

1 Algebra

Quadratic Formula
\[x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}\]

For quadratic equation \(ax^2 + bx + c = 0\)

Binomial Theorem
\[(a + b)^n = \sum_{k=0}^{n} \binom{n}{k} a^{n-k} b^k\]

Where \(\binom{n}{k} = \frac{n!}{k!(n-k)!}\)

Arithmetic Progression
\[S_n = \frac{n}{2}[2a + (n-1)d]\]

Sum of first n terms, where a is first term, d is common difference

2 Trigonometry

Pythagorean Identity
\[\sin^2\theta + \cos^2\theta = 1\]
Sine Rule
\[\frac{a}{\sin A} = \frac{b}{\sin B} = \frac{c}{\sin C} = 2R\]

Where R is circumradius of triangle ABC

Cosine Rule
\[a^2 = b^2 + c^2 - 2bc\cos A\]

3 Calculus

Derivative Rules
\[\frac{d}{dx}(x^n) = nx^{n-1}\]
\[\frac{d}{dx}(\sin x) = \cos x\]
\[\frac{d}{dx}(\cos x) = -\sin x\]
Integration Formulas
\[\int x^n dx = \frac{x^{n+1}}{n+1} + C, n \neq -1\]
\[\int \frac{1}{x} dx = \ln|x| + C\]
\[\int e^x dx = e^x + C\]
Fundamental Theorem of Calculus
\[\int_a^b f(x) dx = F(b) - F(a)\]

Where F'(x) = f(x)

4 Coordinate Geometry

Distance Formula
\[d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2}\]
Section Formula
\[P(x, y) = \left(\frac{mx_2 + nx_1}{m+n}, \frac{my_2 + ny_1}{m+n}\right)\]

Point dividing line segment in ratio m:n

Area of Triangle
\[\Delta = \frac{1}{2} \begin{vmatrix} x_1 & y_1 & 1 \\ x_2 & y_2 & 1 \\ x_3 & y_3 & 1 \end{vmatrix}\]

Mathematics Formula Tips

  • Memorize derivative and integration formulas for quick application
  • Practice applying trigonometric identities in different forms
  • Understand the geometric interpretation of coordinate geometry formulas
  • Learn to derive formulas rather than just memorizing them
  • Create flashcards for quick revision of important formulas

Physics Formulas

Essential formulas for Mechanics, Electrodynamics, Thermodynamics, and Modern Physics with applications.

1 Mechanics

Newton's Second Law
\[F = ma\]

Force equals mass times acceleration

Kinematic Equations
\[v = u + at\]
\[s = ut + \frac{1}{2}at^2\]
\[v^2 = u^2 + 2as\]
Work-Energy Theorem
\[W = \Delta KE = \frac{1}{2}mv^2 - \frac{1}{2}mu^2\]
Gravitational Force
\[F = G\frac{m_1m_2}{r^2}\]

Where G is gravitational constant

2 Thermodynamics

First Law of Thermodynamics
\[\Delta U = Q - W\]

Change in internal energy equals heat added minus work done

Ideal Gas Law
\[PV = nRT\]

Where R is universal gas constant

Efficiency of Heat Engine
\[\eta = 1 - \frac{Q_C}{Q_H}\]

Where Qc is heat rejected, Qh is heat absorbed

3 Electrodynamics

Coulomb's Law
\[F = \frac{1}{4\pi\epsilon_0} \frac{q_1q_2}{r^2}\]

Electrostatic force between two charges

Ohm's Law
\[V = IR\]

Voltage equals current times resistance

Magnetic Force on Moving Charge
\[F = q(\vec{v} \times \vec{B})\]
Faraday's Law
\[\mathcal{E} = -\frac{d\Phi_B}{dt}\]

Induced EMF equals negative rate of change of magnetic flux

4 Modern Physics

Photoelectric Effect
\[E = h\nu = \phi + \frac{1}{2}mv_{max}^2\]

Where œÜ is work function, h is Planck's constant

de Broglie Wavelength
\[\lambda = \frac{h}{p}\]

Where p is momentum, h is Planck's constant

Radioactive Decay
\[N = N_0 e^{-\lambda t}\]

Where Œª is decay constant

Physics Formula Tips

  • Pay attention to units and dimensions in calculations
