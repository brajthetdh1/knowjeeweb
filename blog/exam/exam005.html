Critical Do's and Don'ts

‚úÖ MUST DO's

üìÖ Follow a Strict Time Table

Create and stick to a minute-by-minute schedule. Every hour should be accounted for.

üìù Daily Mock Tests

Take at least one full-length mock test daily under exam conditions.

üìä Analyze Every Mistake

Maintain an error log and ensure you never repeat the same mistake.

üéØ Focus on High-Weightage Topics

Prioritize chapters that carry more marks and are frequently asked.

üí™ Maintain Physical Health

6-7 hours sleep, balanced diet, and light exercise are non-negotiable.

‚ùå STRICT DON'Ts

üö´ Don't Learn New Topics

üìµ Avoid Social Media

üò¥ Don't Compromise Sleep

üìö Avoid New Books/Resources

üí¨ Don't Compare with Others
