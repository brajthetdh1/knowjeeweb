Why Your Body Matters as Much as Your Books

While most JEE aspirants focus only on study materials and mock tests, research shows that physical health factors can improve your performance by 25-40%. Here's what top rankers do differently:

  • Sleep 7-9 hours nightly for memory consolidation
  • Eat brain-boosting foods that enhance focus and retention
  • Exercise regularly to reduce stress and improve cognition
  • Stay hydrated to maintain optimal brain function
