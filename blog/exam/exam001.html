Why Section Order Matters in JEE

The order in which you attempt Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics can impact your final score by 15-25%. This isn't about working harder, but working smarter by leveraging your psychological and academic strengths.

Buffer Time: Always keep 5-10 minutes for review and attempting skipped questions. Never allocate all 90 minutes initially.

The 2-Pass System

First Pass (60-65 minutes)

  • Attempt all sure-shot questions
  • Skip moderately difficult ones
  • Mark questions you're unsure about
  • Maintain steady pace - don't get stuck

Second Pass (20-25 minutes)

  • Return to marked questions
  • Attempt moderate difficulty ones
  • Make educated guesses if needed
  • Review all answers in last 5 minutes

‚è∞ Golden Time Rules

  • 2-minute rule: If stuck for 2 minutes, mark and move on
  • Section timer: Set mental deadlines for each section
  • Buffer principle: Always finish section 5 minutes early
  • Review mandatory: Never submit without final review
