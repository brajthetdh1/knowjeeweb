Why This Dual Strategy Works

Balancing JEE and Board exams is challenging but achievable with the right strategy. This timetable works because:

  • Synergistic learning - Board syllabus covers 70% of JEE Main topics
  • Smart time allocation - Focus shifts based on exam proximity
  • Integrated revision - One study session serves both purposes
  • Reduced stress - Clear roadmap prevents last-minute panic
